What is Siminars?

Siminars is an intuitive, online publishing platform that provides you with all the tools you need to create highy interactive courses and distribute/sell them to your audience.


How does Siminars help me monitize my courses?

We have in-platform credit card and paypal payment systems so you don't have to worry about the collection part. We tie-ups with ClickBank and HasOffers to help you set up an affiliate network of marketers and resellers to sell your content.


Does Siminar allow all these features in the Open (free) Plan?

We wish! But we have infrastructure costs and salaries to pay and hence must charge for our services (but it still is the lowest in the market)! You can try out our platform for free with our Opan Plan and even publish a course, but you wont be able to charge for the course. You can always upgrade to our Retail Plans or Private Plans once you're ready to monitize!

What all does Siminars offer?

With Siminars you can create interactive bite sized courses by including a variety of rich media like video, audio clips and slide shows along with simple text based blog posts. You can also include quizzes, polls, book readings & discussion questions to make learning a lot more fun!


Does Siminars help me maintain my brand?

We understand that maintaining your brand identity is important and hence we provide you with options to customize your course page with your banner and logo.You could also set your own domain name / custom URL and create your own landing page or customize the one we provide to you.


Can I set up a group or a forum for discussions on my course?

We're glad you asked! Siminars has a feature called 'Community' which basically is a forum where your members can interact with each other. You can link your course to an existing community or create a new community, as per your preference!
